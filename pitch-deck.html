Executive Summary (English)

Rafeeq is the mutawwif's field-ops console: it turns the mandatory Nusuk Card into the group's operating system — attendance, missing persons, medical emergencies, and crowd routing — from one tablet.

Track 3 — Smart Crowd Management

  • Monitor: live density map, 45-bead group pulse, Nusuk scan
  • Analyze: 6-hour forecast, Jamarat ingress/egress flow, AI copilot
  • Manage: safe reroute, mutawwif broadcast, reunification network
  • Integrate: Baseer, Nusuk, Nusuk Business, SEHA Virtual, SDAIA bands

Why indispensable?

Baseer sees the macro crowd. Nusuk serves the pilgrim. Nusuk Business is back-office. Only Rafeeq connects national decisions to 45 pilgrims in front of the supervisor — with health, safety, and audit trail in one second.
